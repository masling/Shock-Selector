# Page Copy Drafts (EN)

## Purpose

This document provides reusable English draft copy for the main website pages. It is based on the local sourcebook and is meant to accelerate future page implementation.

Use these drafts as:

- a starting point for UI content
- a content checklist for missing sections
- a shared language base between design, content, and development

Do not assume every sentence is final. Anything marked `verify later` should be reviewed against official catalog or sales material before launch.

### Adjustable family draft

`Adjustable Shock Absorbers are designed for applications with changing loads or variable motion conditions, where damping can be tuned to improve deceleration behavior and end-stop control.`

### Non-adjustable family draft

`Non-adjustable Shock Absorbers are designed for repeatable machine motion, where simple installation, consistent stopping behavior, and long service life are more important than manual tuning.`

Supporting note:

`Self-compensating / non-adjustable models are well suited to stable, repetitive operating conditions.`

### Heavy-duty family draft

`Heavy Duty Shock Absorbers are intended for severe impact duties such as cranes, rail systems, and automated warehouse protection, where large energy absorption and equipment safety are the first priorities.`

### Super-long-life family draft

`Super Long Life Shock Absorbers are built for high-frequency machine motion where uptime, repeatability, and long service intervals are critical.`

### Heavy-industry buffers draft

`Heavy Industry Buffers are intended for large moving structures and high-mass industrial equipment where terminal protection must remain reliable under demanding conditions.`

### Vibration-isolator family draft

`Wire Rope Vibration Isolators are designed for equipment protection in environments where vibration isolation, shock resistance, and corrosion resistance must work together.`

### Special-vibration / compound-isolator note

If these lines become public pages later, position them as:

- specialized vibration-isolation solutions
- shock-resistant composite isolation
- qualification-sensitive or harsh-environment applications
